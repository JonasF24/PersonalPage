Stock Screenr

A live, browser-based stock research dashboard for identifying high-quality investment opportunities across three distinct strategies — powered by real-time Finnhub data.

↗ Launch App ⌨ View on GitHub

Overview

Stock Screenr is a single HTML file that runs entirely in your browser. It pulls live quotes, fundamentals, and news headlines directly from Finnhub and applies three independent stock screens to surface the best opportunities within each strategy.

All screening criteria are verified against the most recent earnings reports — not estimates. Built with plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript with no frameworks or build step required.

Live quotes via Finnhub REST API with WebSocket real-time tick updates
Earnings-verified screening — criteria checked against actual reported figures
Insights panel — per-stock investment thesis and risk factors on every passing name
Post-Market News Feed — auto-fetches headlines for any selected stock after 4:00 PM ET
ETF price strip — live quotes for SCHD, SPY, VOO, and VTI as market benchmarks
Dual clocks — local time and US Eastern Time displayed simultaneously

The Three Screens

Low Risk Value
Targets businesses with consistent, above-average growth trading at a low price relative to expected growth (PEG < 1). Screens for 5Y revenue growth >50%, ROE >15%, D/E <1, and positive free cash flow.
Buffett Stocks
Classic value-compounder criteria. Targets profitable businesses with strong ROE (>20%), conservative balance sheets (D/E 0–0.5), positive dividends, and P/E under 25 — inspired by fundamental quality investing.
Dividend Growth
Top-500 US companies with dividend yield >1.5%, 5+ consecutive years of increases, ROE >15%, D/E <1, and payout ratio <70%. Stocks ranked by dividend yield, highest to lowest.
